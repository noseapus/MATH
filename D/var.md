**Definition:** The variance of a [random variable](/D/rvar) $X$ is defined as the [expected value](/D/mean) of the squared deviation from its [expected value](/D/mean):

$$ \label{eq:var}
\mathrm{Var}(X) = \mathrm{E}\left[ (X-\mathrm{E}(X))^2 \right] \; .
$$
