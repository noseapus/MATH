**Definition:** Consider measured data $y$ and some unknown latent parameters $\theta$. A statement about the distribution of $y$ given $\theta$ is called a generative model $m$:

$$ \label{eq:gm}
m: \, y \sim \mathcal{D}(\theta) \; .
$$
