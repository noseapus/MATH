**Definition:** The standard deviation $\sigma$ of a [random variable](/D/rvar) $X$ with [expected value](/D/mean) $\mu$ is defined as the square root of the [variance](/D/var), i.e.

$$ \label{eq:std}
\sigma(X) = \sqrt{\mathrm{E}\left[ (X-\mu)^2 \right]} \; .
$$
