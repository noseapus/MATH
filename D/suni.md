**Definition:** Let $X$ be a [random variable](/D/rvar). Then, $X$ is said to be standard uniformly distributed, if $X$ follows a [continuous uniform distribution](/D/cuni) with minimum $a = 0$ and maximum $b = 1$:

$$ \label{eq:suni}
X \sim \mathcal{U}(0, 1) \; .
$$
