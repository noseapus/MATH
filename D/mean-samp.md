**Definition:** Let $x = \left\lbrace x_1, \ldots, x_n \right\rbrace$ be a [sample](/D/samp) from a [random variable](/D/rvar) $X$. Then, the sample mean of $x$ is denoted as $\bar{x}$ and is given by

$$ \label{eq:mean-samp}
\bar{x} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \; .
$$
