**Definition:** Let $X$ be a [random variable](/D/rvar). Then, $X$ is said to follow a Bernoulli distribution with success probability $p$

$$ \label{eq:bern}
X \sim \mathrm{Bern}(p) \; ,
$$

if $X = 1$ with [probability](/D/prob) $p$ and $X = 0$ with [probability](/D/prob) $q = 1-p$.
